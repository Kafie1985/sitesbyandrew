This is My journey

This is a Site About my journey Going from Welder To Web developer

I always knew I wanted to Learn to program and I tried several times from python books to java books but life always seems to get in the way. I joined the Army to get money for collage and i ended up Being a Apache Helicopter Crew Chief I deployed to Afghanistan in January While I was gone my wife gave birth to our 2nd child. So needless to say when I got back from Deployment I need to work.

I Got a Job at A company that builds Storage tanks for the oil and gas, and mining fields. meanwhile I progressed towards getting my VA disability claim handled. I noticed as I was working that the work was really making my disability's worse

I consulted my Va benefits and found that I was able to apply for the Va Voc Rehab Program and was approved and met with my counselor and we came up with a plan and a goal. PROGRAMMING I wanted to enroll in Western Governors University but it takes a while for all the Pieces to fall into place So I started learning from Udemey I went through a few Courses and learned That i did LOVE Coding.

I got Approved to the Va Program and Learned that I learned that I needed to get more expirence to be accepted into my degree program. I Had to complete the COMPTIA A+ Certification to prove prior IT Experience

So I started working on that I went back to the tried and tested Udemy and found 4 courses By: Mike Myers I started with the 901 class and worked through it and then I Bought the his book it was Amazingly helpful and really helped me learn the material I worked through the practice questions and took the test. AND PASSED Haha. it felt great like my first real step in the Tech World so I buckled Down and got the 902 done about the same way. I got the Cert and was admitted into My program August 1 2017.
